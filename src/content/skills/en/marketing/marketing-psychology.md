## Introduction

When the user wants to apply psychological principles, mental models, or behavioral science to marketing.

- Leverages principles like social proof, scarcity, and anchoring
- Analyzes consumer behavior and decision-making
- Uses behavioral science to nudge user actions

## Applicability

Ideal for marketers and product designers looking to optimize conversions by understanding and influencing how their users think.

## Highlights

Bridges the gap between academic behavioral science and practical marketing application, making psychology actionable.

Use this skill to analyze why people buy, how to frame an offer better, or when incorporating concepts like loss aversion into your copy.
