## Introduction
This skill serves as a specialized marketing framework for AI, transforming it into a domain expert. It addresses the common challenge of users not knowing where to start or how to structure their marketing efforts by providing systematic, proven methodologies.

## Core Concept
Leverage structured methodologies and pre-defined frameworks to generate actionable, context-aware marketing strategies and assets, moving beyond generic advice to tailored, practical solutions.

## Skill Design Evaluation
- **Strengths of SOP Integration**: Provides a systematic framework for applying psychological concepts to marketing, moving from identifying the model to explaining the "why" and providing specific applications.
- **Potential Limitations**: Psychological principles can easily be misapplied or overused; relying on an AI to interpret nuanced behavioral science might sometimes result in superficial recommendations.
- **What Makes a Good Skill**: It actively enforces an ethical boundary by requiring the AI to suggest how to implement these psychological nudges ethically, preventing manipulative or "dark pattern" marketing.
