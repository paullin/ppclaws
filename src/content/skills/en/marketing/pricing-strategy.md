## Introduction

When the user wants help with pricing decisions, packaging, or monetization strategy. Use this whenever someone is figuring out what to charge or how to structure their plans.

## Features

- Develops pricing tiers, packaging, and monetization models
- Advises on freemium vs. free trial strategies
- Helps determine value metrics and willingness to pay
- Structures pricing pages (annual vs. monthly, per-seat)

## Applicability

Essential for SaaS founders and product marketers who are defining their initial pricing or planning a price increase.

## Highlights

Provides a systematic approach to pricing that aligns what you charge with the actual value your users receive.

## Usage

Call this skill when asking 'how much should I charge', 'should I offer a free plan', or when designing your pricing page.
