## Introduction

You MUST use this before any creative work - creating features, building components, adding functionality, or modifying behavior. Explores user intent, requirements and design before implementation.

## Features

- Helps users turn ideas into fully formed designs and specifications.
- Enforces a hard gate to require project context exploration and design approval before coding.
- Provides a systematic questioning process to clarify requirements, constraints, and success criteria.
- Proposes multiple approaches with trade-offs and outputs a final design document.

## Highlights

- Sets a "HARD-GATE" to prevent blind development before requirements are clear.
- Includes an Anti-Pattern warning emphasizing that even simple projects need a design phase.
- Features a structured checklist and process flow to ensure completeness of steps.
