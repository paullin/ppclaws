## Introduction

When the user wants to optimize post-signup onboarding, user activation, first-run experience, or time-to-value. Use this whenever users are signing up but not sticking around.

## Features

- Optimizes post-signup onboarding flows
- Improves user activation rates and time-to-value
- Designs empty states and onboarding checklists
- Analyzes why new users fail to activate

## Applicability

Perfect for products experiencing high drop-off rates immediately after signup, or when users fail to reach the 'aha moment'.

## Highlights

Targets the critical first-session experience, combining UX best practices with psychological triggers to improve retention.

## Usage

Trigger this skill when you notice low activation rates or want to redesign the first-run experience for new users.
